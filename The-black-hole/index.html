[ The Hungry Blackhole: Pupu]

Scientific Name:

Emotional Gravitational Collapse Object

Discovered by / at:

Yanhan Zhu, 2025/3

Family:

Emotigravidae--very rare in Universe, their behavior and gravity depend on their emotions!

Habitat:

"Resonance" Zone in deep space--This region is filled with emotional resonance particles, which help the Emotigravidae celestial bodies stabilize their emotions and provide the energy they need to survive.

Appearance:

A cute, floating jelly-like black hole, surrounded by colorful light waves and starry dust, with expressive glowing eyes and a tiny playful "mouth."

Key Characteristics:

  • Calm Pupu: if you don't bother Pupu, it will stay calm and enjoy the starry dust around it.
  • A little angry Pupu: if you keep on bothering Pupu, it'll get slightly angry and send gravitational waves that change the color of the starry dust beside it.
  • Erupting Pupu: Pupu get truly angry after continuous botherings, it will erupt and unleash emotional particles.
  • ...

What we know so far about Pupu? Pupu is a unique and special black hole that belongs to a family known as Emotigravidae (the "emotional gravity family"). Unlike normal black holes, Pupu is alive with emotions and interacts with its surroundings based on how it feels. Pupu feeds on emotional resonance particles that stabilize its feelings.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature Carefully touch this creature and you'll see the gravitational waves; Try harder you'll see its' emotional changes; Be careful not to touch it too many times or it will erupt!(You'll regret it!)
